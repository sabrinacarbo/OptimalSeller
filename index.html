How OS Works

Optimal Seller was created to assist not only individuals who have experience with selling product, but who are also new to selling. Our goal is to provide the user with the ideal price, category and keywords necessary to sell your item through ecommerce.

To use this app, you will need to select the Get Started button at the top right-hand side of the screen. This will take you to a page with a search box. We then take the keywords you have entered and search the Etsy site to find as many relevant matches as possible. **Remember, the more specific, the better the returned information.
